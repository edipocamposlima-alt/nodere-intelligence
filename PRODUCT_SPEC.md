# Nodere Intelligence Product Spec

## Positioning

Nodere Intelligence is a commercial intelligence SaaS that discovers companies with weak digital presence, audits their Google/marketing readiness and turns those gaps into sales-ready opportunities.

## Target Users

- Traffic managers.
- Google Ads consultants.
- Digital agencies.
- SDR teams.
- Closers.
- Local business growth consultants.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

1. Find companies by city, segment, keyword and digital weakness.
2. Detect missing or poor Google Business Profile, Google Ads and conversion setup.
3. Score opportunities by commercial potential.
4. Generate a diagnosis and sales pitch.
5. Contact the lead through WhatsApp or Inbox.
6. Track the lead in CRM.
7. Measure team and pipeline performance.

## Main Modules

### Dashboard

Executive overview of total companies, qualified leads, digital failures, average score, opportunity ranking and pipeline.

### Search

Advanced lead discovery by city, segment, keyword, region, maturity and missing assets.

### Scanner

Saved searches, recurring scans, queue status and commercial alerts.

### Google Intelligence

Google Business Profile and Google Ads readiness analysis, including reviews, posts, Q&A, conversions, assets, recommendations and local performance.

### Company

Company-level profile with public data, diagnosis, score, website preview, audit findings, playbook and timeline.

### CRM

Kanban pipeline from new lead to closed/lost.

### Automation

Generated WhatsApp/email messages, rules and integration roadmap.

### Inbox

Centralized sales conversations, quick replies, AI response generation and SLA actions.

### Copilot AI

Close probability, recommended services, ROI simulator and proposal generation.

### Diagnosis

Client-facing print/PDF diagnostic report.

### Reports

CSV/PDF/Excel export concept, ranking, opportunities by city and niche.

### Admin

Users, invitations, permissions by tab, security, WhatsApp settings and API connectors.

### Billing

Plans, credits, usage and invoices.

### Performance

Operator ranking, goals, forecast and productivity heatmap.

## Scoring Model

Recommended production score components:

- Google Business Profile completeness.
- Review volume and rating.
- Website availability and speed.
- WhatsApp visibility.
- GTM/GA4/conversion tracking.
- Google Ads activity.
- SEO/local search quality.
- Segment value and estimated ticket.
- Response probability based on CRM history.

## Production Integrations

- Google Places API.
- Google Maps APIs.
- Google Business Profile APIs.
- Google Ads API.
- PageSpeed Insights API.
- GA4/Admin APIs where authorized.
- WhatsApp Cloud API.
- Stripe.
- Firebase/Auth.js.
- PostgreSQL.
- Redis queue.

## Success Metrics

- Leads discovered per user.
- Qualified lead rate.
- Diagnosis-to-meeting conversion.
- Meeting-to-proposal conversion.
- Proposal-to-close conversion.
- Revenue per operator.
- Cost per qualified opportunity.
- Average response time.
- Credits consumed per closed deal.
